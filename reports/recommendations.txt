ENGAGEMENT OPTIMIZATION RECOMMENDATIONS
============================================================
Based on statistical analysis of 30 videos and 633 comments

1. Prioritize LONG-FORM content (5+ min). It outperforms short content by 106% in viral coefficient.

2. Target a Relatability Rate of 55%+ in comments. Videos above this threshold scored 9.7x higher in viral coefficient.

3. Use 'to anyone feeling lonely and feels like they don’t belong | ...' as a content template — it achieved the highest viral coefficient (39.05) with 86% relatability.

4. Top-performing content leans emotionally positive/uplifting (avg sentiment: 0.33). Hopeful, resolution-focused framing performs better than purely sad content.

5. Use direct address or question-style titles (e.g., 'Feeling lonely?'). They outperform generic statement titles.
